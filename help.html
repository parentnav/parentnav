Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I reset my password?
If you need to reset your password, go to the login page and click on the "Forgot Password" link. You will receive an email with instructions to reset your password.

2. How can I update my child's information?
To update your child’s information, navigate to the “Profile” section, select your child's profile, and click on “Edit Profile”. Make the necessary changes and save.

3. How do I contact emergency services?
In case of an emergency, you can find the “Emergency Contact” page in the sidebar menu. This will allow you to dial your stored emergency contact numbers directly from the app.

4. How can I track my child's medical records?
To track your child’s medical records, go to the “Medical Records” section in the sidebar. Here, you can view and upload new medical reports.

5. How do I connect with other parents?
You can connect with other parents by navigating to the “Connect with Parents” page. From there, you can find parents in your area and send them a message to start a conversation.
